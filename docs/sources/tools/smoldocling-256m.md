SmolDocling 256M — IBM Research's smaller-scale predecessor to Granite-Docling 258M. Same architectural family (Idefics3-based) but pre-Granite-LLM. Part of the Oct-2025 local-document-VLM wave (per brainstorm v2 §7.1). Cited in HORUS as a **Layer-1 cohort entry** primarily for ablation: comparing SmolDocling vs Granite-Docling within the same family isolates the impact of the Granite-LLM upgrade. May be deprecated by the time experiments run; secondary priority unless ablation needs surface.
