Fattural — **tooling**, not a dataset. Companion training-data generation tool within the ZUGFeRD project ecosystem (alongside the Mustang Project). Generates synthetic ZUGFeRD/Factur-X invoices for training and evaluation. Cited in HORUS as a **secondary synthetic-data generator** alongside `docs/sources/tools/mustang-project.md`. Originally listed under datasets in brainstorm v2 §15 ("verify whether dataset or tooling" — Q4 of the M2D.5 issue #12 Q&A round). Resolved 2026-05-13: confirmed it's a generation tool, not a curated corpus. Moved from `docs/sources/datasets/fattural.md` to `docs/sources/tools/fattural.md` per ADR-002 source classification. Specific generation capabilities (templates, configurability, output formats) to be confirmed at deep-read or first-use evaluation.
