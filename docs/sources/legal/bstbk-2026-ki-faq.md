BStBK FAQ "KI im steuerberatenden Berufsstand" (Bundessteuerberaterkammer, January 2026). Authoritative national-Kammer guidance on AI tooling for German Steuerberater. Cited in HORUS as the **primary contemporaneous legal source** (per brainstorm v2 §7.6) for the §203-StGB / §62a-StBerG compliance argument: public AI services (ChatGPT and similar) cannot be used for Mandantendaten without sufficient contractual protection. Establishes that DSGVO compliance alone is NOT sufficient — a separate §203-StGB-aware Berufsgeheimnis-Verpflichtung in Textform per §62a StBerG is required. Anchors HORUS's legal-positioning claim that, as of early 2026, local inference is the only legally watertight option for Steuerberater on Mandantendaten.
