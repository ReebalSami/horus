ZUGFeRD specification (and XRechnung sibling) — German implementation of the EN 16931 European e-invoicing standard. Two formats: **XRechnung** (pure XML, public-sector invoicing) and **ZUGFeRD** (hybrid PDF/A-3 with embedded XML, B2B invoicing). Per brainstorm v2 §7.5: Germany made B2B e-invoicing **mandatory to receive** as of 1 January 2025; **fully mandatory to issue** by 2027. Cited in HORUS as the **primary legal/standard source** anchoring (a) the practical relevance of the German B2B invoice domain — every Steuerberater client will be exchanging ZUGFeRD-format invoices by 2027; (b) the structured-ground-truth-via-embedded-XML evaluation methodology — ZUGFeRD's hybrid format is what makes label-free evaluation possible. Specific FeRD specification URL to be located at deep-read; Mustang Project is the most accessible secondary-source landing page.
