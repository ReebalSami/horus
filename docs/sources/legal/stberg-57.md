§ 57 StBerG — *Allgemeine Berufspflichten* (Steuerberatungsgesetz). Defines the general professional duties of Steuerberater, including Verschwiegenheit (confidentiality), independence, and Eigenverantwortlichkeit. Cited in HORUS via brainstorm v2 §7.6 as the **statutory basis for the Verschwiegenheitspflicht** that § 62a StBerG operationalises for external-provider scenarios. § 57 establishes the obligation; § 62a defines the gates through which external providers (including AI tooling) may pass while preserving it; § 203 StGB is the criminal-law sanction if the obligation is breached. Together § 57 + § 62a StBerG + § 203 StGB form the German Steuerberater Berufsgeheimnis-Trinität that constrains HORUS's architectural commitments.
