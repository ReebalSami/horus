OpenAI Data Processing Addendum — the contractual framework OpenAI offers to API / Team / Enterprise customers for DSGVO compliance (Auftragsverarbeitung per Art. 28 DSGVO). Per brainstorm v2 §7.6: OpenAI offers EU data residency since February 2025 and EU inference residency since January 2026 for Enterprise/API tiers. Cited in HORUS as **vendor-contract context for the cloud-baseline column**: the DPA solves DSGVO compliance, but does NOT solve § 203 StGB / § 62a StBerG (no separate Berufsgeheimnis-Verpflichtung in Textform offered as of early 2026; § 62a Abs. 4 fails on US-provider foreign-provider clause regardless). Documents the gap between "DSGVO-compliant" and "Steuerberater-§203-compliant" — central to the thesis's chapter-1 legal argument.
