§ 203 StGB — *Verletzung von Privatgeheimnissen* (Berufsgeheimnis-Verletzung). German criminal-code provision protecting confidentiality of information entrusted to specific Berufsgruppen including Steuerberater, Rechtsanwälte, and Ärzte. Penalty: up to 1 year imprisonment or a fine; plus berufsrechtliche sanctions up to Berufsverbot. Cited in HORUS as the **central criminal-law constraint** anchoring the thesis's privacy-first architectural commitment (per brainstorm v2 §7.6): unauthorised disclosure of Mandantendaten to external service providers (cloud AI APIs) constitutes a § 203 violation unless the provider has been formally contracted under § 62a StBerG with comparable secrecy protection. Defines the concrete legal cell where local inference is the only watertight option as of 2026.
