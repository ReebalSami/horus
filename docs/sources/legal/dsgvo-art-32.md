Art. 32 DSGVO — *Sicherheit der Verarbeitung* (Security of Processing). Mandates appropriate technical and organizational measures (TOMs) for personal-data processing under DSGVO/GDPR; reference standard for evaluating whether a vendor's processing posture is "DSGVO-compliant." Cited in HORUS as the **central DSGVO obligation** applicable to all personal-data handling in HORUS-relevant scenarios. Per brainstorm v2 §7.6: DSGVO-compliance is "solvable for cloud APIs" via DPAs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure) — Art. 32's TOM requirements are the criterion against which those DPAs are judged. The thesis's legal argument is that Art. 32 alone is insufficient for German Steuerberater work — § 203 StGB + § 62a StBerG add a sectoral overlay that current cloud DPAs do not address. Art. 32 is necessary but not sufficient.
