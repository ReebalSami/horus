Berghaus et al. 2025 (Fraunhofer IAIS, arXiv 2509.04469). Benchmarked GPT-5, Gemini 2.5, and Gemma 3 on invoice-parsing tasks. Cited in HORUS as the **direct literature gap** (per brainstorm v2 §7.5): the paper benchmarks proprietary cloud VLMs on invoice processing but does NOT include any of the Oct-2025 specialized open-source document VLMs (Granite-Docling, olmOCR-2, Nanonets-OCR2, dots.ocr, MinerU 2.5, etc.). HORUS fills this gap: open-source local-inference document VLMs evaluated on the same German B2B invoice scope under the §203-StBerG legal frame that excludes cloud baselines. Also cited as the source for the inv-cdip (Tobacco subset) reference (per brainstorm v2 §15 Datasets).
