Donut (Document Understanding Transformer) introduced the first end-to-end OCR-free transformer for document understanding tasks (information extraction, classification, VQA), trained on synthetic + real document images. ECCV 2022. Cited in HORUS as the **historical reference** for the OCR-free paradigm — the architectural precedent the Oct-2025 VLM cohort (Granite-Docling, olmOCR-2, Nanonets-OCR2, dots.ocr, MinerU 2.5, etc.) extends and replaces. Building Layer 1 around Donut in 2026 would be like building an NLP thesis around word2vec in 2023 (per brainstorm v2 §7.1).
