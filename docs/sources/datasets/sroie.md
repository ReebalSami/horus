SROIE (Scanned Receipts OCR and Information Extraction) — ICDAR 2019 Robust Reading Challenge dataset for receipt-image OCR and structured-information extraction. ~1000 scanned receipt images with bounding-box and key-value annotations (company, date, address, total). Cited in HORUS as a **classical OCR/IE benchmark** (per brainstorm v2 §15 Datasets); secondary to CORD-v2 (which is more modern and Hugging-Face-hosted). Useful as a backward-compatibility check: how do modern document VLMs perform on a ~7-year-old benchmark that older OCR-pipeline systems were designed against? Performance ceiling on SROIE should be near-saturated for any competent 2025-era VLM.
