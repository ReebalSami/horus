FUNSD (Form Understanding in Noisy Scanned Documents) — Jaume et al. 2019. ~200 noisy scanned forms with annotations for form-field detection, text recognition, and entity-relationship grouping. Cited in HORUS as a **classical form-understanding benchmark** (per brainstorm v2 §15 Datasets). Relevant if the German-invoice scope expands toward form-like layouts (e.g., German-tax forms, structured invoices with explicit field labels). Smaller than CORD-v2 and SROIE; primarily useful for entity-relation/grouping evaluation rather than raw extraction accuracy. Secondary to the German-invoice corpora.
